What is a Web3 Wallet?

A Web3 wallet like MetaMask is a digital wallet that allows you to interact with blockchain applications. It's similar to a digital passport for the decentralized web.

Election Facts

  • The 2023 Pakistani general election was held on February 8, 2023.
  • Imran Khan's party, Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI), was not allowed to use its electoral symbol.
  • Despite this, PTI-backed independent candidates won the most seats in the National Assembly.
  • The Election Commission of Pakistan (ECP) delayed announcing results, raising concerns about manipulation.
  • International observers and human rights organizations raised concerns about the fairness of the election.

Timeline of Events

  • April 2022
    Imran Khan was removed as Prime Minister through a no-confidence vote.
  • May 2022
    Khan began nationwide protests calling for early elections.
  • November 2022
    Khan survived an assassination attempt during a political rally.
  • August 2023
    Khan was arrested and sentenced on multiple charges, which many view as politically motivated.
  • February 2024
    Despite PTI-backed candidates winning the most seats, a coalition government was formed without them.
