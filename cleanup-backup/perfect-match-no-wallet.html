Election Facts

  • The 2023 Pakistani general election was held on February 8, 2023.
  • Imran Khan's party, Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI), was not allowed to use its electoral symbol.
  • Despite this, PTI-backed independent candidates won the most seats in the National Assembly.
  • The Election Commission of Pakistan (ECP) delayed announcing results, raising concerns about manipulation.
  • International observers and human rights organizations raised concerns about the fairness of the election.
