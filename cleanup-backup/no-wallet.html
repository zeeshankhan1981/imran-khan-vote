Election Facts: February 8, 2024

Landslide Victory

On February 8, 2024, Imran Khan's Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) party won a landslide victory in the general elections, securing over 180 seats in the National Assembly despite facing unprecedented obstacles.

Imprisoned During Elections

Imran Khan was imprisoned during the elections on politically motivated charges, yet his party still managed to win the majority of seats, demonstrating his overwhelming public support.

Form 47 Manipulation

Despite PTI's clear victory, the Election Commission of Pakistan manipulated Form 47 results to deny PTI their rightful seats, leading to widespread protests across Pakistan.

International Recognition

Multiple international election observers and human rights organizations acknowledged the electoral fraud and recognized Imran Khan's party as the true winner of the 2024 elections.
